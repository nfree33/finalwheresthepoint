How to Play

'Where's the Point?' is based off a 2019 viral game that took the nation by storm. It's fun, easy, and can be played anywhere!

The rules are simple. Players take alternating turns on offense and defense:

  • Offense: 'Point' either up, down, left, or right, using the directionals on the keypad. As the offensive player, you are attempting to point in the direction the CPU has looked.
  • Defense: 'Look' in a specific direction before the CPU points. Look by using (W) for up, (A) for left, (S) for down, and (D) for right. As the defender, you are hoping your look direction is not matched to where they point.
  • If the offensive player is successful, they win the round. First player to win three rounds wins the game.
